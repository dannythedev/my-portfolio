Swift Capture!

How to Use.

Swift Capture! is a screenshot tool designed to capture regions of your screen quickly and efficiently, with few clicks as possible.

Select a region of your screen, press 'S' anytime you want a screenshot!

With its intuitive interface and easy-to-use features, Swift Capture! makes capturing regional screenshots a breeze, only a key-press away! In the example above, 5 consecutive screenshots are captured in a flash!
